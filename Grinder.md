The Grinder can uses: 60 kW to process various materials into alternative forms, including 
* ores and ingots -> raw metal
* logs -> planks
* blaze rods -> blaze powder
* bones -> bonemeal
* cobblestone -> sand
* stone -> gravel
* gravel -> flint.
