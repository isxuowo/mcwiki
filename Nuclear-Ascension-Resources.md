## Ores

![Uranium Ore](https://i.imgur.com/HlIFYGX.png?1)

Uranium Ore: Rare Ore that spawns between Y-1 and Y-24. Fire and Uranium do not mix, so uranium needs to be refined in a Grinder from Age of Machines.
